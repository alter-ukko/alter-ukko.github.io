About

I am a software engineer and architect with a focus in the last 7 years on data engineering.

I’ve worked in a variety of industries, including transportation, energy, videoconferencing, occupational health and human resources. I’ve also done work in the criminal justice non-profit space. You can download my resumé here.

I have been programming computers for a long time (my first computer was a TRS-80 Model I), but since my AP CompSci class in high school (Pascal on a Commodore SuperPET), I haven’t received any formal education in Computer Science. I studied Philosophy in college, which has served me well, apart from the fact that I’m too philosophical about everything – which isn’t really college’s fault.
